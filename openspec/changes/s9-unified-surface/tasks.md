      manifest.rs::git_head_sha`) — that artifact is never drift-checked.
      Tested: `digest12_is_deterministic_and_twelve_hex_characters`,
      `digest_kinds_over_an_empty_git_root_is_the_em_dash_placeholder`,
      `compute_reads_policy_yaml_when_present`,

      byte_identical` asserts `!content.contains("generated_at")`;
      `tests/check_gate.rs::check_reports_no_drift_after_the_commit_that_
